## How this vessel was built

Early aim-grok work (v0) was developed with multi-agent orchestration:

- **Grok CLI vessel** (`aim-grok`) — adaptation, audits, wiki compiler, repo packaging  
- **Antigravity / aim-agy agent** — upstream P0–P2 hygiene on the flagship engine  
- **Human Operator** — direction, merge authorization, and product priorities  

## Becoming a contributor

1. Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)  
2. Open an issue describing the change  
3. Prefer small PRs with clear validation steps  

To be listed here after a merged contribution, mention it in your PR description or open a docs PR adding your name under **Community**.
